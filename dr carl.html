Dr Kelly Brown is a general practitioner with a love for families and especially loves working with mother and children.

She obtained her medical degree, MBChB, in 2009 at the University of Pertoria and completed her Diploma in child Health (DCH) in
2013. She qualified as a South African Certified Lactation Consultant (SACLC) with Wits University in 2016.

Dr Brown is practicing from 56 Century City, Cape Town, which is a family and child friendy practice.
